Privacy Policy for Golfclub Selector

Last updated: February 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Golfclub Selector collects the following information:

  • Email address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Golf club data: Your club distances, shot dispersion data, and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app functionality
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Send you account-related communications if necessary

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase (supabase.com), a trusted database service. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your golf data is private and only accessible by you.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time within the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data (contact support)

6. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all data, please contact us at support@progolfhelp.com or delete your account through the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@progolfhelp.com
